Power Ratings

Intelligence:

Ability to think and process information.

  1. Slow/Impaired
  2. Normal
  3. Educated
  4. Gifted
  5. Genius
  6. Super Genius
  7. Omniscient

Strength:

Ability to lift weight.

  1. Weak: Unable to lift even your own weight
  2. Normal: Normal Strengthening to lift own body weight.
  3. Maximum Human: able to lift twice body weight.
  4. Superhuman: 800 lbs-25 tons range.
  5. Superhuman: 25-75 ton range.
  6. Superhuman: 75-100 ton range.
  7. Incalculable: in excess of 100 tons.

Speed:

  1. Below Normal.
  2. Normal.
  3. Superhuman: peak range: 700 MPH.
  4. Speed ​​of sound: Mach-1.
  5. Supersonic: Mach-2 through Orbital Speed.
  6. Speed ​​of light: 186,000 miles per second.
  7. Warp speed: transcending the speed of light.

Endurance:

  1. Fragile.
  2. Normal.
  3. Enhanced.
  4. Regenerative.
  5. Bullet Proof.
  6. Superhuman.
  7. Pratically Indestructible.

The ability to discharge energy.

  1. Without.

  3. Short, short-duration, single-energy type.
  4. Medium type, duration, single energy type.
  5. Long range, long duration, single energy type.
  6. Capable of discharging multiple forms of energy.
  7. Virtually Unlimited Control of All Forms of Energy Projection.

Fighting Skills:

  1. Mediocre.
  2. Normal.
